Introduction to Us

Customer's smile is our achievement after the relocation we do. Our team consists of members who understand the value of attachments with the belongings. Integrity, honesty, transparency are the values identified by customers in our team.

So, How do we do all of this?

We are present on Justdial, LinkedIn, Google Plus, Facebook and through email. It all starts with your enquiry with us. Whenever you enquire with us, our sales Representative/Surveyor would visit the premises at a time convenient for you to meet at your home. We have maintained 100% accuracy in material description and gauging the vehicle, labour and packing material requirement since the time we started our first assignment.

Upon your permission we will click the photographs of the household to be packed and moved for insurance purpose only. When we are done with the survey we email you the quote within an hour of survery.

Doing this paperless work, reduces the use of paper and this is the smallest part we can do as a business organisation to save trees.

Our surveyor would understand the complete requirements from you, would also make notes of any special instructions and handling, and would accept one of the choices you make for time and date when vehicle should report and time required to relocate after explaining possible options available for relocation.

Our measures for customer satisfaction are quality of packing, delivery time which are second to none, ratings of our are available with our promotion partner M/s Justdial on their website.
